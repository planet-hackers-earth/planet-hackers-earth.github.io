One of the most cost effective avenues for mitigiating the effects of climate change is through the use of statospheric aerosols. Use of this powerful technique requires public and international cooperation, so it is our mission to build free and open source models of statospheric aerosols for all to scrutinize, as well as to develop better molecules for atmospheric dispersal.
